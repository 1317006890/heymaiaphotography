My name is Halle Harrison,

----I run Hey Maia Photography! I graduated Skyview High School in May of 2023. I have lived in Nampa, Idaho my entire life and grew up boating around the San Juan Islands, as well as showing animals with my siblings at the local county fairs. During the start of my photography journey, I decided to name my business after my border collie-golden retriever mixed puppy, Maia. In my spare time I love to crochet and create things. Photography is one of my many passions and I love the people I get to meet and work with all while creating beautiful images.
