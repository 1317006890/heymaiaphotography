About me

Hi! My name is Halle Harrison and I run Hey Maia Photography. I graduated from Skyview High School in May 2023.

I have lived in Nampa, Idaho, my entire life and grew up boating around the San Juan Islands, as well as showing animals with my siblings at the local county fairs. My past four years in High School, I have been working in and helping manage Columbia High School’s greenhouse while being a member of the Nampa FFA. My botany and horticulture classes gave me experience of running a greenhouse and taught me floral design. During the school year, we would practice different styles of arrangements and sell our work. At the end of the year we would have a big sale for our greenhouse. When I'm not at school or taking photos, I love to crochet and create things.

I come from a family of six (seven if you count my dog☺️),and am the youngest of four siblings; There are my parents, Taunia and Tony, my two sisters, Tea (the eldest) and Tricia, and my brother, David. We had a family dog, Maia, that had been with us since 2010. She and I were very close and spent pretty much all of our time together. She was a fan favorite and when I first started my photography business, I named it after her. She passed away October 25, 2022, but her name still lives on.

Photo Credit: David Cook

I specialize in shooting Senior Photos/Portraits, Couples, and Small Families. I love how my photography allows me to reconnect with and meet so many people that I may not have otherwise. Working with clients to give them the photos they are looking for and encouraging confidence makes me really love what I do.
